Krillin

Overview

Krillin (クリリン) is a major supporting character from the Dragon Ball series created by Akira Toriyama. He is one of Earth's strongest humans and is best known as Son Goku's closest friend and long-time ally.

Early Life

Krillin began his journey as a martial arts student under Master Roshi, training alongside Goku. Though initially competitive and insecure, he quickly developed into a loyal companion through shared struggles and battles.

Personality

Krillin is known for being:

  • Brave despite his fears
  • Loyal to friends and family
  • Humorous and light-hearted
  • Selfless in dangerous situations

Although often portrayed as comic relief, Krillin repeatedly proves his courage by standing against enemies far stronger than himself.

Role in the Story

Throughout the Dragon Ball series, Krillin fights alongside Goku against powerful villains including the Saiyans, Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu. His death at the hands of Frieza directly triggers Goku's first transformation into a Super Saiyan.

Martial Arts Training

Trained by Master Roshi, Krillin combines martial arts skill with strategic thinking, allowing him to hold his own even against overwhelming odds.

Powers and Abilities

Krillin possesses several notable abilities, including:

  • Flight using ki energy
  • Kamehameha energy wave
  • Solar Flare (Taiyōken)
  • Destructo Disc (Kienzan)
  • Exceptional battle tactics

Despite being human, Krillin is widely regarded as the strongest Earthling in the Dragon Ball universe.

Family and Relationships

Krillin eventually marries Android 18, with whom he has a daughter named Marron. He later retires from full-time combat and works as a police officer while still assisting during major crises.

Later Development

In Dragon Ball Super, Krillin continues to support Earth's defenders and even participates in the Tournament of Power, proving his growth as both a fighter and a person.

Legacy

Krillin represents:

  • The strength of ordinary humans
  • True friendship and loyalty
  • Courage in the face of fear
  • One of Dragon Ball's most beloved characters

Though not the strongest warrior, Krillin's heart, bravery, and dedication make him an irreplaceable part of the Dragon Ball story.
